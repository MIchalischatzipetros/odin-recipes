Pastitsio! A greek pasta oven dish, similar to lasagna

This recipe is all about building everything from scratch

  1. For the meat filling:

    • 750 g ground beef
    • 2 onions
    • 1 clove(s) of garlic
    • 1 teaspoon(s) thyme, fresh
    • 4 tablespoon(s) olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon(s) granulated sugar
    • 2 tablespoon(s) tomato paste
    • 100 ml red wine
    • 400 g canned tomatoes
    • 1 teaspoon(s) cinnamon
    • 2 beef bouillon cubes
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 1/2 bunch parsley, finely chopped

  2. For the béchamel sauce:

    • 100 g butter
    • 100 g all-purpose flour
    • 1 λίτρο γάλα πλήρες
    • salt
    • pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon(s) ground nutmeg
    • 100 g gruyere cheese, grated
    • 3 egg yolks, from medium eggs

  3. for the pasta:

    • 300 g bucatini pasta
    • 30 ml olive oil
    • 1/2 bunch parsley
    • 1/2 bunch thyme
    • 2 medium sized eggs
    • 200 grams feta cheese

  4. For the plate setting

    • 50 g gruyere cheese, grated
